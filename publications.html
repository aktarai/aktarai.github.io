PhD Thesis

My PhD thesis focused on the application of deep learning techniques for wavefront sensing in adaptive optics. It presents a novel approach to real-time wavefront reconstruction and improves the performance of adaptive optics systems.

The thesis includes a detailed study of machine learning techniques applied to wavefront sensing, along with experimental validation. It is available for download in PDF format and can also be accessed through INFLIBNET.
