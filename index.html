Biography

I am a Postdoctoral Research Fellow in the Department of Physical Sciences at Indian Institute of Science Education and Research (IISER), Berhampur, India, working on wavefront sensing using deep learning for adaptive optics applications. I obtained my PhD in Physics from the Advanced Centre of Research in High Energy Materials (ACRHEM), School of Physics, University of Hyderabad, India in 2024.

My research interests include Laser Produced Plasma Diagnostics, Laser-Induced Breakdown Spectroscopy (LIBS), Raman Spectroscopy, and applications of Machine Learning and Deep Learning in Laser Spectroscopy and Adaptive Optics.

Research Areas & Interests

  • Laser Produced Plasma
  • Laser Induced Breakdown Spectroscopy (LIBS)
  • Raman Spectroscopy
  • Wavefront Sensing in Adaptive Optics
  • Machine Learning and Deep Learning
